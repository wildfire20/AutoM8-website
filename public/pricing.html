FAQ

Common Questions

How does payment work?

Custom projects are split into milestones: typically 40% upfront, 30% at midpoint, and 30% on delivery. School systems are billed monthly based on active students.

How long does a project take?

Timelines depend on scope. Simple tools take 2-4 weeks, full platforms 4-8 weeks, and enterprise solutions 8-16 weeks. We provide detailed timelines before starting.

What support is included?

All projects include post-launch support (3-12 months depending on tier). This covers bug fixes, minor adjustments, and technical assistance. Extended support plans available.

Who handles hosting?

We can host your solution on our AWS infrastructure (included in school plans, optional for custom projects) or deploy to your preferred hosting environment.

Do I own the code?

Yes. Upon final payment, you receive full ownership of all custom code developed for your project. We provide complete source code and documentation.

Can solutions scale?

Absolutely. We build on scalable cloud architecture. As your business grows, your system can handle increased load without rebuilding from scratch.
