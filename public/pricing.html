FAQ

Common questions.

How does payment work?

Projects are split into milestones: 40% upfront, 30% at midpoint, 30% on delivery. Flexible for larger projects.

How long does a project take?

Simple tools: 2–4 weeks. Full platforms: 4–8 weeks. Enterprise: 8–16 weeks. We'll give a firm timeline upfront.

What support is included?

All projects include 3–12 months support covering bug fixes, security patches, and technical assistance.

Do I own the code?

Yes. You receive full ownership of all custom code — complete source code and documentation is yours forever.
