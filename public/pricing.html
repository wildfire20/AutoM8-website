Frequently Asked Questions

How is billing calculated?

Billing is based on the number of active students in your system. Admin and teacher accounts are completely free. You only pay for student accounts.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees! We include onboarding, training, and system configuration in all our plans.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

What about data security?

All data is hosted on secure AWS servers with encryption, regular backups, and industry-standard security protocols.

Do you offer training?

Yes! We provide comprehensive training for administrators and teachers as part of all our plans.

What if I need support?

We offer support via email, WhatsApp, and phone. Premium plans get priority support with faster response times.
